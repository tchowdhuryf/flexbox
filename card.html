Lapras

It ferries people across the sea on its back. It may sing an enchanting cry if it is in a good mood.

Snorlax

This gluttonous Pokémon eats constantly, apart from when it's asleep. It devours nearly 900 pounds of food per day.

Charmander

The flame on its tail shows the strength of its life-force. If Charmander is weak, the flame also burns weakly.

Eevee

Its ability to evolve into many forms allows it to adapt smoothly and perfectly to any environment.

Chikorita

It loves to bask in the sunlight. It uses the leaf on its head to seek out warm places.

Pikachu

When it is angered, it immediately discharges the energy stored in the pouches in its cheeks.

Wartortle

Wartortle's long, furry tail is a symbol of longevity, so this Pokémon is quite popular among older people.

Jigglypuff

When its huge eyes waver, it sings a mysteriously soothing melody that lulls its enemies to sleep.
